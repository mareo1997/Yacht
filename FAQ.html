Frequently Asked Questions

1. What kind of yachts are available? That's up to you we offer sail, electrical and manned boats.

2. How many people can travel with you? We recommend up at most 5 people.

3. Am I qualified to captain my own yacht? That will depend on your sailing experience but if you feel that you will need help we will set you up with a skipper.

4. How old must you be to rent a yacht? You must be 18 years or older to rent a yacht.

5. Are children welcome? Yes, children are welcome.

6. How do I book a vacation? You can book your vacation online. Simple fill in your destination, dates and yacht on our web-site 24 hours a day.

7. What documents are required? You must present a valid passport unless you are a US citizen traveling within the US.

8. Is there Wi-Fi on board? Yes, all our yachts come with free Wi-Fi.
