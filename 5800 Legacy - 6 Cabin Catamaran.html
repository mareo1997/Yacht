Explore the islands in plush comfort aboard The Crewed 5800 Legacy Edition. This spacious, air-conditioned boat features five guest cabins-all with private en-suite baths-and room for up to 10 guests.

Wake up to breathtaking waterfront vistas and roam about the gorgeously spacious floor plan both inside and out. When you're ready, meet with the captain who will help you plan the day's adventure and kick back as your personal chef whips up gourmet meals for everyone aboard.

The 5800 Legacy Edition set the standard with an innovative split-level accommodation plan featuring two roomy cabins on the main deck and four more (including crew quarters) below. The saloon features a large dining table with plush wrap-around seating and a wet bar. Step outside the saloon to dine in the sweeping aft cockpit as the hostess caters to your every need and you kick back on teak outdoor furniture. Or, venture up to the fly bridge and enjoy a 360-degree view your tropical surroundings with stylish lounge seating, a wet bar/refrigerator and grill to help your crew craft a bevy of palate-pleasing cocktails and open-air dining.
